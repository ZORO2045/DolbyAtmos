  • No need for MotoCore
  • Magisk & KSU Supported

Installation Guide

  • • Remove any other Dolby modules.
  • • Download the DolbyAtmos module and flash it using Magisk or KSU.
  • • Reboot your device.
  • • Open the Dolby Atmos app and customize your settings.
