Installation Guide

To install Moto Dolby Atmos:

  1. Remove any other Dolby modules with different name.
  2. Download DolbyAtmos module from below and flash it using Magisk or KSU.
  3. Reboot your device.
  4. Congratulations! DolbyAtmos has been installed on your device.
  5. Open the Dolby Atmos app and customize your audio settings to your preference.
